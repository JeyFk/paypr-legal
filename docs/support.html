Common questions

Where is my data stored? On your device, and synced through your own iCloud account if you're signed in. We never see it. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I manage my subscription? Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then Subscriptions. You can also reach this from Paypr's Settings ▸ Manage subscription.

How do payments work? Paypr does not process payments. You pay people however you normally do; in Paypr you simply mark an amount as paid and it updates the balance you're tracking.
