Questions, answered

How do I track what I owe my nanny?

Add your nanny once with their hourly rate, then log the hours they work each day by dragging across a timeline or tapping a quick entry. Paypr keeps a running balance of what you owe, so payday is never a guess.

Is Paypr free?

Yes. You can track one person free forever. Paypr Pro unlocks unlimited people, weekly/monthly/yearly summaries and CSV export for $2.99/month or $7.99 once for lifetime.

Can I use Paypr for a babysitter, tutor or cleaner?

Yes. Paypr works for anyone you pay by the hour — nanny, babysitter, sitter, tutor, house cleaner, housekeeper or any helper. Set each person's rate and track their hours and payments separately.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your records stay on your device and sync through your own private iCloud account. No ads, no data selling, and analytics never include names, amounts or balances.

Does Paypr help at tax time?

Paypr Pro exports clean CSV records of hours worked and payments made per person — handy for keeping tidy nanny-tax records.
