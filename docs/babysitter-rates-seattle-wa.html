Babysitter & childcare rates in Seattle, WA

Updated 2026-07-17 · BLS 2024 data

In the Seattle-Tacoma-Bellevue, WA area, childcare workers earn a median of $19.32 per hour, with most paid between $17.93 and $22.22. Use this as the local anchor for what a regular sitter or nanny is worth, then adjust for the factors below.

These are childcare-worker wage figures — what employed and regular sitters earn locally. Occasional, on-demand date-night babysitting usually pays more per hour because it's short-notice and flexible. Treat this as the floor, not the ceiling.

What raises the rate in Seattle, WA

  • More kids: add about $2–$5/hour per additional child.
  • Infants & multiples: pay more than one school-age child.
  • Overnights, holidays & late nights: expect a premium.
  • Extras: cooking, driving, bath-and-bed nudge it up.
